Time Event Description
3:00 PM Big Tech Gathering Introduction Learn more about the day and get to know about the best pre-professional student technology club on campus, the CMUBTG!
3:15 PM - 4:00 PM Who's Hiring? Startup Showcase presented by the UEA The Undergraduate Entrepreneurship Association is presenting hiring startups, showcasing their technology and people.
4:05 PM - 4:35 PM Alumni Spotlight: Spencer Burleigh on Startups and Y Combinator Spencer Burleigh is the co-founder and Chief Financial Officer of Rent the Backyard. Rent the Backyard pays for, builds, and manages "accessory dwelling units" in Bay Area backyards. Rent the Backyard participated in the startup accelerator Y Combinator's Summer 2019 batch and is in the process of building their first units now. He will be speaking live from San Francisco about the exciting process of moving a startup from idea to reality, and getting funded in the process.
4:40 PM - 5:10 PM Resume Workshop Carnegie Mellon counselors and career services have a ton of advice on general resumes, but what about tech resumes? How should they look like, what should they say, and what do you leave out? Learn what it takes to form a coherent and modern resume for the tech industry, and how to fine-tune your presentation for various roles and positions, so that you can get past the automated screener.
5:15 PM - 6:00 PM Interview Preparation Tech interviews are notorious for their rigor and high level of preparation. Learn about general guidelines for tackling any technical interview and showing your best self on that important day. Whiteboarding advice will also be included.
6:05 PM - 7:00 PM Internship Panel Former and returning employees to Microsoft, Uber ATG, Spotify, and Amazon share their experiences on their journey through Carnegie Mellon and the tech industry, and offer their own advice.

What is Big Tech Gathering

The CMUBTG is excited to present Big Tech Gathering, a technology conference for Carnegie Mellon students to learn more about and prepare for careers in the technology industry. Big Tech Gathering brings together students from a variety of backgrounds and professional experiences to convene and learn.

We'll see you there.
