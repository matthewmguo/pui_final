Frequently Asked Questions

When and where is BTG? ↓

BTG will be held on January 24, 2021 at Simmons Auditorium in the Tepper School of Business building. The Tepper building is located on Carnegie Mellon University at 4765 Forbes Ave, Pittsburgh, PA 15213.

What's the schedule for the day? ↓

Please visit the schedule page.

Do I need to know how to program to go to BTG? ↓

Absolutely not. If you're curious about what it's like working in tech, then you should come to BTG. Some workshops are designed for job candidates with a technical background, but there's plenty to check out!

What is Big Tech Gathering

The CMUBTG is excited to present Big Tech Gathering, a technology conference for Carnegie Mellon students to learn more about and prepare for careers in the technology industry. Big Tech Gathering brings together students from a variety of backgrounds and professional experiences to convene and learn.

We'll see you there.
